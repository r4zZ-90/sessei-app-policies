Sessei — 節制 · 摂生

Sessei is the philosophy of active biological governance — treating health not as a passive state, but as a lifelong discipline of structured self‑restraint. It demands the intentional regulation of fasting, hydration, and nutritional intervals to keep the physical vessel in perfect metabolic alignment. True optimization does not come from erratic bio‑hacking, but from the rhythmic, conscious execution of foundational habits. By mastering the daily cycles of intake and restriction, you reclaim control over your biological trajectory. Ultimately, Sessei is the quiet armor built through consistency, transforming raw discipline into sustained vitality.

節制 · 摂生

In Japanese, 節制 (sessei) means moderation, temperance, abstinence. 摂生 (sessei) means care of one's health. Both readings reinforce the same idea: deliberate self‑governance — the brand work done before any copy is read.
